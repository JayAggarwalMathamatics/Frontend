Homemade Fries

Since I was little, this recipe has been with me. With its crunch in every bite, and its fresh ingredients, this has been my favorite snack for a long time now. Now, I will share the recipe to you.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Water
  • Ice
  • Oil

Instructions

  1. Peel, cut, and wash the potatoes into fry shapes.
  2. Put the potatoes in ice-cold water for 10 minutes.
  3. Take it out and wipe it with a paper towel.
  4. Put oil in a pan, then put the first batch of fries in at medium heat and cook till golden brown.
  5. Take the fries out on a paper towel, and put it on a plate, adding salt and black pepper.
  6. Enjoy!
